My suggestion

Before writing a patent application, prove the idea scientifically.

Build a prototype that can answer questions like:

Does this reduce unnecessary rerouting?
Does it reduce driver uncertainty or anxiety?
Does it improve ETA accuracy?
Does it increase user trust compared with conventional navigation?
Can you quantify improvements over existing routing?

If you can demonstrate measurable improvements, you gain three things:

Stronger evidence for a patent application.
Material for an academic paper.
A compelling story for investors and customers.

Those three together are often much more powerful than any one of them alone.
